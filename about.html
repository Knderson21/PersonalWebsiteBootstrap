About Krista

Krista spent her childhood growing up in southeast Michigan, then as a young adult ventured up north to attend Northern Michigan University. Afterwards, she landed a job at Amrock, formerly known as Title Source, one of the Rock Family of Companies located downtown Detroit, Michigan. She worked there for 7 years and enjoyed her role as a Senior Examiner. She is now studying computer programming at Grand Circus's FullStack C# Bootcamp. In her freetime, Krista collects and repairs arcade machines and rents them to events as a part of the Detroit based arcade, Psychic Drive.
